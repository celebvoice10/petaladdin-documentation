# Appointment Details Screen

The `Appointment Details` page is the best place to review a single visit and continue its workflow.

## What the page shows

The page can include:

- date
- time
- type
- status
- doctor
- department
- room
- equipment
- pet parent
- pet
- species
- title
- notes
- cancellation reason

## Main actions

Depending on status and permissions, the page can show:

- `Check In`
- `Edit`
- `Create Consultation`
- `Open Consultation`
- `Open Vaccination`

## Workflow panel

The workflow section shows the appointment lifecycle:

- scheduled
- confirmed
- checked in
- in consultation
- completed
- no show
- cancelled

## When to use this page

Open it when:

- reception is checking in a patient
- a doctor is starting the consultation
- staff need to confirm room or department assignment
- the clinic needs to review notes or a cancellation reason
