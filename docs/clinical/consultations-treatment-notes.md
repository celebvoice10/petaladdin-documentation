# Consultations and Treatment Notes

Clinical teams should record visit details clearly so the next person reviewing the case can understand what happened without guesswork.

## Before documenting

- confirm you have opened the correct patient profile
- review the most recent visit history
- check whether there are existing reminders or follow-up instructions

## During the consultation

Record the information your clinic requires, such as:

- reason for visit
- examination findings
- treatment or medication given
- advice shared with the owner
- recommended follow-up

## After the consultation

1. Review the notes for clarity.
2. Confirm any treatment items that should flow to billing.
3. Add follow-up or revisit instructions if needed.
4. Save the record before moving to the next patient.

## Documentation tips

- use consistent wording for common visit outcomes
- keep notes specific and clinically useful
- avoid leaving draft information incomplete at shift change
