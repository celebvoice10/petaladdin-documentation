# Reports

Reports help clinic leaders and operations teams understand what is happening across appointments, billing, and overall activity.

## Reports often used by clinics

- appointment volume by day or provider
- billing and collections summaries
- outstanding balances
- patient activity trends

## Using reports effectively

- choose a clear date range before running the report
- confirm that staff are entering appointments and billing data consistently
- compare totals against daily operations when reviewing exceptions

## Practical use cases

- monitor busy clinic hours
- review revenue trends
- identify missed charges or payment gaps
- support operational planning
