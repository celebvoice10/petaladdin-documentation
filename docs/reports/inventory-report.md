# Inventory Report

The `Inventory Report` gives a stock-risk view across low stock, out-of-stock items, near-expiry batches, and expired stock.

## What users can do here

- set the near-expiry day threshold
- review inventory risk metrics
- jump into affected product records

## Key metrics on this page

The report can show:

- stock items
- low stock
- out of stock
- near expiry
- expired

## Main sections

The page may include:

- low-stock products
- out-of-stock products
- near-expiry batches
- expired stock batches

## When to use this report

Use this report when:

- preparing purchase orders
- planning stock adjustments for expired items
- checking reorder pressure
- reviewing batch expiry risk
