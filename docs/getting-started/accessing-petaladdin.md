# Accessing Petaladdin

Petaladdin is typically accessed through a web browser provided by your clinic or hospital.

## Before you begin

- make sure you have the correct login URL
- confirm that your account has already been created
- keep your work email or username ready

## Signing in

1. Open the Petaladdin login page provided by your clinic.
2. Enter your username or email address.
3. Enter your password.
4. Select `Sign In`.

## First-time access

If you are logging in for the first time, your clinic may ask you to:

- change your temporary password
- verify your contact details
- review your assigned role and access

## If you cannot log in

- check for typing errors in your email or password
- make sure Caps Lock is off
- ask your clinic administrator whether your account is active
- review [Common Issues](../troubleshooting/common-issues.md)
