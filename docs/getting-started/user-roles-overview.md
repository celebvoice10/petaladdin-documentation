# User Roles Overview

Petaladdin is easier to manage when each team member understands which parts of the system they use most often.

## Common roles

### Reception and front desk

Typical tasks include:

- booking appointments
- checking patients in and out
- updating owner contact details
- helping with invoice handoff and payment confirmation

### Doctors

Typical tasks include:

- reviewing patient history
- recording consultation notes
- updating treatment details
- checking visit outcomes before check-out

### Nurses and support staff

Typical tasks include:

- preparing patient information
- assisting with treatment workflow
- updating operational notes where required

### Billing and operations staff

Typical tasks include:

- reviewing invoices
- confirming payment status
- checking outstanding balances
- monitoring daily reports

### Administrators

Typical tasks include:

- clinic setup and maintenance
- user access and permissions
- support and troubleshooting
- policy review and process oversight

## Best practice

Give each person the lowest level of access they need. This keeps workflows clear and helps reduce accidental changes to critical settings.
