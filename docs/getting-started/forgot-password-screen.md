# Forgot Password Screen

Use the `Forgot Password` screen when you can no longer sign in with your current password.

## What the screen includes

- email field
- `Send Reset Link` button
- `Back to login` link

## How to request a reset link

1. Open `Forgot password?` from the login page.
2. Enter the staff email linked to your account.
3. Select `Send Reset Link`.
4. Check your email for the reset message.

## Best practice

Use the same email address shown in your `My Account` profile. If the email is outdated or inaccessible, ask a clinic administrator to review your user record.
