# Common Issues

This page covers a few issues clinic teams may encounter during daily use.

## I cannot sign in

Try the following:

- confirm that you are using the correct login link
- re-enter your password carefully
- ask an administrator whether your account is active
- try again from another supported browser if needed

## I cannot find a patient

- check the spelling of the pet or owner name
- search using a partial name if available
- verify that a duplicate record was not created under a variation

## My invoice looks incorrect

- confirm the services or items added to the bill
- review quantities, pricing, and any discount applied
- compare the invoice against the appointment or visit details

## A page does not load properly

- refresh the page
- sign out and sign back in
- test another supported browser
- report the issue to your clinic administrator with the exact steps you followed
