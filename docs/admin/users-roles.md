# Users and Roles

Petaladdin should be configured so each team member has access that matches their responsibilities.

## Why roles matter

Roles help protect sensitive information and reduce accidental changes to billing, reporting, or administrative settings.

## Common user groups

- reception or front desk
- veterinary doctors
- nurses or support staff
- billing or operations staff
- administrators

## Recommended access approach

1. Give each user a unique account.
2. Assign the lowest level of access needed for their work.
3. Review role assignments whenever responsibilities change.
4. Disable access promptly when a staff member leaves.

## Administrative routine

Administrators should periodically:

- review active users
- remove unused accounts
- confirm role assignments remain accurate
- verify that sensitive functions are limited to trusted staff
