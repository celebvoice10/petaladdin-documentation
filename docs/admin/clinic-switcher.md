# Clinic Switcher

The `Clinic` switcher in the top bar lets users change their active clinic when they have access to more than one clinic.

## What the switcher does

- shows the current clinic in the header
- lists available clinics assigned to the user
- switches the active clinic immediately after selection

## How to switch clinics

1. Open the clinic dropdown in the top bar.
2. Select the clinic you want to work in.
3. Wait for the `Switching...` status to finish.

After the change, dashboards, appointments, inventory, billing, and reports update to the selected clinic context.

## When to use this

Use the clinic switcher when:

- working across multiple branches
- reviewing reports for another clinic
- creating staff, appointments, or invoices in a different clinic context

## Important note

Always confirm the active clinic before scheduling appointments, receiving purchases, or posting invoices. Many records are saved against the current clinic.
