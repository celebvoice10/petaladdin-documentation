# Settings

The `Clinic Settings` page controls clinic-level defaults for consultations, appointments, billing tax behavior, and product tax rules.

## What users can do here

- set default weight and temperature units
- define appointment slot durations
- choose billing tax mode
- manage tax groups
- manage taxes
- assign product tax rules
- import or export clinic tax maps

## Main settings sections

The page is organized into expandable sections:

- `Clinical Units`
- `Appointment Slots`
- `Billing Preferences`
- `Tax Groups`
- `Taxes`
- `Product Tax Rules`

## Clinical and appointment defaults

Clinic teams can set:

- default weight unit: `kg` or `lbs`
- default temperature unit: `C` or `F`
- slot duration for consultation, vaccination, surgery, grooming, follow-up, and emergency appointments

These defaults affect day-to-day scheduling and consultation entry.

## Billing preferences

The page supports two tax modes:

- `Tax Exclusive`
- `Tax Inclusive`

Use the mode that matches how your clinic stores product and service prices.

## Tax setup tools

If tax features are enabled, the page can also manage:

- tax groups
- tax records
- product-level tax overrides
- bulk category or product-type rules
- tax-map CSV export and import

## Product tax rules

Use product rules when a product needs a tax treatment that differs from the normal clinic default.

The page supports:

- product search by name, SKU, or barcode
- sale or purchase tax context
- bulk rules by category or product type
- review of active product overrides

## Best practice

Only change tax settings when the clinic has confirmed billing rules, because these settings affect product pricing, POS, quotations, and invoices.
