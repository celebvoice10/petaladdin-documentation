# Clinic Admin Workflow

This guide helps clinic administrators use Petaladdin to monitor operations, support staff access, and keep clinic settings accurate.

![Dashboard overview screen](/img/screenshots/dashboard-overview-screen-annotated.jpg)

Quick guide:

- `1` changes the dashboard focus to match the area the admin is reviewing.
- `2` shows appointment load and queue pressure for the day.
- `3` highlights preventive care activity that may need follow-up.
- `4` opens quick actions for common operational tasks.

## Daily route

1. Start from the dashboard and review the clinic's operational snapshot.
2. Check whether staff, appointments, billing, and inventory are moving normally.
3. Resolve access, settings, or clinic-data issues that block the team.
4. Review reports, receivables, and low-stock risks.
5. Close the day by checking exceptions and planning follow-up actions.

## Core admin responsibilities

### 1. Monitor live clinic activity

Use [Dashboard Overview](../dashboard/dashboard-overview.md), [Appointments Report](../reports/appointments-report.md), and [Clinical Report](../reports/clinical-report.md) to track daily operational health.

### 2. Support people and access

Use [Users](../admin/staff-user-management.md), [Users and Roles](../admin/users-roles.md), [Permission Roles](../admin/permission-roles.md), and [Access Audit](../admin/access-audit.md) to manage staff and permissions safely.

### 3. Maintain clinic settings

Use [Clinics](../admin/clinic-management.md), [Settings](../admin/clinic-settings.md), and [Clinic Switcher](../admin/clinic-switcher.md) when multiple clinics or configuration changes are involved.

### 4. Review financial and inventory health

Use [Billing Report](../reports/finance-report.md), [Inventory Report](../reports/inventory-report.md), [Managing Medicines and Stock](../inventory/managing-medicines-stock.md), and [Purchases](../inventory/purchase-management.md) to support operational decisions.

### 5. Watch communication and subscriptions

Use [Communication](../admin/communication-logs.md) and [Subscription](../admin/subscription-management.md) when reminders, package limits, or renewals affect clinic operations.

## Weekly review checklist

- review inactive or duplicate staff accounts
- check receivables and refund patterns
- confirm low-stock or near-expiry items are being handled
- review subscription limits before staff or clinic slots run out
- spot-check whether workflows still match clinic policy

## When to escalate

Escalate to the platform owner or technical support when:

- multiple users lose access unexpectedly
- reports are missing large blocks of data
- clinic settings changes create billing or tax inconsistencies
- subscription limits block clinic operations unexpectedly
