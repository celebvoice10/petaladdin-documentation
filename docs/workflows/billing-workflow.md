# Billing Workflow

This guide covers the main Petaladdin tasks handled by billing staff, cash counter users, and operations staff during a normal shift.

![POS checkout workflow screen with callouts](/img/screenshots/pos-checkout-workflow-screen-annotated.jpg)

Quick guide:

- `1` confirms the bill type and active customer context.
- `2` adds items to the bill through product search and row entry.
- `3` reviews pricing, quantity, and line totals before checkout.
- `4` applies payment collection details and checkout actions.
- `5` completes the POS bill once the payment is confirmed.

## Daily route

1. Review the invoice queue, POS bills, and pending collections.
2. Complete patient billing after check-out or create over-the-counter sales in POS.
3. Collect payment and confirm invoice status.
4. Handle reprints, corrections, or refunds when needed.
5. Close the shift with a billing and receivables review.

## During the shift

### 1. Complete regular patient billing

Use [Billing](../billing/invoice-management.md), [Create Invoice Screen](../billing/create-invoice-screen.md), and [Edit Invoice Screen](../billing/edit-invoice-screen.md) for patient-linked invoices.

### 2. Use POS for counter sales

Use [POS Checkout Workflow](../billing/pos-checkout-workflow.md) for quick sales that do not require the full visit workflow.

### 3. Confirm payment status

Use [Invoices and Payments](../billing/invoices-payments.md) to review whether invoices are paid, partially paid, or still outstanding.

### 4. Handle exceptions

Use [Invoice Print Screen](../billing/invoice-print-screen.md), [Refund Workflow](../billing/refund-workflow.md), and [Invoice Refund Record](../billing/invoice-refund-record.md) when a bill must be reissued, refunded, or reviewed.

### 5. Review promotions and plans

Use [Coupons](../billing/coupon-management.md) and [Memberships](../billing/membership-plan-management.md) when discounts or membership benefits affect the bill.

## End-of-day checks

- review unpaid invoices and partial collections
- confirm refunded invoices are documented correctly
- compare billing totals with daily reports
- flag mismatches between consultation items and billed items

## When to escalate

Tell an administrator or supervisor when:

- invoice totals do not match products or services on the record
- discounts, coupons, or memberships calculate incorrectly
- refunded invoices still appear as collectible
- repeated tax or pricing mismatches appear across bills
