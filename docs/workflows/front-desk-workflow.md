# Front Desk Workflow

This guide brings together the most common tasks handled by reception and front-desk staff during a normal clinic day.

## Before opening

- review today's appointment schedule
- check for cancellations, urgent cases, or doctor changes
- confirm the team can access Petaladdin normally

## During appointment hours

### 1. Schedule and update visits

Use [Managing Appointments](../appointments/managing-appointments.md) to book, move, or cancel visits.

### 2. Check patients in

Use [Patient Check-In and Check-Out](../appointments/check-in-check-out.md) to manage arrivals and front-desk handoff.

### 3. Support record accuracy

Use [Patient Profiles](../patient-records/patient-profiles.md) when you need to confirm client details or correct duplicate information.

### 4. Coordinate billing handoff

Use [Invoices and Payments](../billing/invoices-payments.md) to support check-out and payment tracking.

## End-of-day routine

- review uncompleted appointments
- confirm cancelled visits were recorded properly
- flag unpaid invoices or unusual billing issues
- prepare notes for the next shift if anything remains unresolved

## When to escalate

Tell an administrator or supervisor when:

- users cannot log in
- patient information appears duplicated or missing
- billing totals do not match the visit details
- the calendar shows repeated scheduling problems
