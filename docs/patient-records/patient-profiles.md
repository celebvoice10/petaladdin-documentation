# Patient Profiles

A patient profile keeps important pet and owner information together so teams can provide safe, efficient care.

## Typical information stored

- pet name, species, breed, and sex
- age or date of birth
- client or owner details
- visit history
- treatment notes
- vaccination or medical reminders

## Creating a patient profile

1. Open the patient records section.
2. Select the option to add a new patient.
3. Enter the pet's details and link the correct owner.
4. Save the profile.

## Updating records

Keep patient information current whenever:

- contact details change
- new medical notes are recorded
- treatment history needs to be attached to a visit
- duplicate entries are discovered and resolved

## Good record habits

- enter information immediately after the visit when possible
- use consistent naming and spelling
- confirm owner contact details during check-in
