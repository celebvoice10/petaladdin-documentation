# Product Details Screen

The product details page is the best place to review a single item's stock, pricing, tax profiles, and recent movement history.

![Product details screen](/img/screenshots/product-details-screen.jpg)

## What the page shows

The screen can include:

- clinic
- category
- SKU
- barcode
- product type
- behavior flags
- HSN
- expiry
- current stock
- sale price
- cost price
- sales tax profile
- purchase tax profile
- available batches
- recent stock movements

## Available batches section

For stock items, the page may show:

- batch number
- expiry date
- available quantity
- received quantity
- near-expiry warning

## When to use this screen

Open it when:

- checking stock before billing
- reviewing near-expiry batches
- confirming tax or product type setup
- investigating recent stock movement
