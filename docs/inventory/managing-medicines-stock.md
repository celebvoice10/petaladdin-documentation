# Managing Medicines and Stock

Clinics need a clear view of medicines, consumables, and low-stock items so treatment and billing stay aligned.

## Common stock tasks

- review available medicine quantities
- monitor frequently used consumables
- update stock after dispensing or treatment
- identify items nearing low-stock levels

## Recommended routine

### Daily

- review critical items at the start of the day
- record any important stock adjustments

### Weekly

- check fast-moving items
- confirm quantities against clinic process
- review unusual usage patterns

## Operational tips

- use consistent item names
- update stock as close to real time as possible
- flag discrepancies early instead of waiting until month-end

## Why this matters

Good stock discipline improves:

- treatment readiness
- invoice accuracy
- purchasing decisions
- audit confidence
