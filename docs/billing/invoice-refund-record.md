# Invoice Refund Record

The `Invoice Refund` record shows the details of a refund already posted against an invoice.

## What the page shows

The refund record can include:

- refund number
- clinic
- linked invoice
- refund date
- status
- pet parent
- reason
- refunded line items
- tax amounts
- subtotal
- discount reversal
- membership reversal
- coupon reversal
- tax total
- refund total

## Additional sections

The page may also show:

- promotion reversals
- tax reversal summary

## When to use this page

Open the refund record when:

- finance needs a refund audit trail
- a client asks for the refund reference
- you need to confirm the amount and reason posted
